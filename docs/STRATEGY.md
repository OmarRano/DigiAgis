# DigiAGIS Platform Strategy

## 1. Platform Identity
**Name Ideas:**
*   **DigiAGIS** (Current) - Professional and descriptive.
*   **AgisLink** - Highlights the bridge between users and the government database.
*   **VeriLand** - Focuses on the core value: Verification.
*   **TrustPlot** - Emphasizes security in the land market.

## 2. Business Model Canvas
*   **Key Partners**: AGIS Certified Agents, Surveyors, Legal Verification Experts.
*   **Key Activities**: Agent Vetting, Listing Verification, Smart Matchmaking.
*   **Value Proposition**: Scam-free land deals for buyers; consistent workflow and brand building for agents.
*   **Customer Relationships**: Automated trust scores, secure deal initiation rooms.
*   **Customer Segments**: Abuja Land Buyers/Investors, Real Estate Brokers, Developers.
*   **Channels**: Mobile Web App, WhatsApp Integration, Social Media Ads.
*   **Cost Structure**: Platform maintenance, verification staff, marketing.

## 3. Revenue Model
| Stream | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Verification Commission** | 10-15% service fee on every verification job assigned to an agent. |
| **Agent Subscriptions** | Pro ($15/mo) and Elite ($50/mo) tiers for unlimited listings and priority leads. |
| **Featured Listings** | Paid promotion for property developers to appear at the top of search. |
| **Document Guidance** | Premium concierge service for land application and title transfer. |

## 4. Launch Strategy (Abuja Market)
1.  **Beta Phase (Month 1)**: Onboard 50 certified AGIS agents with free 6-month Pro subscriptions.
2.  **Listing Blitz (Month 2)**: Focus on 3 key areas (Maitama, Asokoro, Gwarinpa) to ensure high-quality initial data.
3.  **Trust Campaign (Month 3)**: Content marketing focused on "How to avoid land scams in Abuja" using the platform.
4.  **Partnerships**: Collaborate with Abuja-based legal firms to endorse the verification workflow.

## 5. Pitch Presentation (Investor Focus)
*   **The Problem**: $XXX Million lost annually to property scams in Nigeria due to manual, opaque verification processes.
*   **The Solution**: A digital bridge that initiates deals only after document verification by certified experts.
*   **Traction**: (Prototype ready) Codebase supports multi-role dashboards and GIS integration.
*   **The Ask**: Funding for backend infrastructure, marketing, and official partnership integration.
